## Do/Don't Examples

### Opening Lines

❌ Don't: "In today's competitive marketplace, businesses need to stand out."
✅ Do: "Your competitors are boring. Here's how to not be."

❌ Don't: "It's important to understand the fundamentals before diving in."
✅ Do: "Skip the theory. Here's what actually matters."

### Explaining Concepts

❌ Don't: "This methodology leverages proven frameworks to optimize outcomes."
✅ Do: "This works because it matches how people actually make decisions."

### Making Arguments

❌ Don't: "One could argue that this approach might potentially yield better results."
✅ Do: "This approach wins. Here's why."

### Calls to Action

❌ Don't: "Consider implementing these strategies in your next campaign."
✅ Do: "Try this on your next launch. You'll see the difference immediately."
