# Platform Transformations

How to adapt content for each platform.

---

## Twitter/X

### Format Options

**Single Tweet**
- 280 characters max
- Best for: Hot takes, quotes, questions
- Hook in first line essential

**Thread**
- 10-15 tweets typical
- Best for: Teaching, stories, breakdowns
- First tweet is the hook, last tweet is CTA

**List Thread**
- Numbered format (1/, 2/, 3/...)
- Best for: Steps, tips, examples

### Transformation Rules

1. Lead with strongest point (no preamble)
2. One idea per tweet
3. Use line breaks for readability
4. End with clear CTA or question
5. First tweet must hook independently

### Twitter Template: Article → Thread

```
1/ [Hook from article - strongest insight or contrarian take]

2/ [Context or problem setup]

3-8/ [Main points, one per tweet]

9/ [Story or example]

10/ [Summary or key takeaway]

11/ [CTA: Follow for more, check link, etc.]
```

### Character Optimization

- "And" → "&"
- Remove filler words
- Use contractions
- Numbers over words (7 not seven)
- Cut unnecessary qualifiers

## LinkedIn

### Format Options

**Text Post**
- 3,000 characters max
- 1,300 characters optimal
- Best for: Insights, opinions, stories

**Carousel**
- Up to 20 slides (PDF format)
- Best for: Step-by-step, lists, frameworks

**Article**
- Long-form within LinkedIn
- Best for: Deep dives, thought leadership

### Transformation Rules

1. First line is everything (hook above "see more")
2. Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences)
3. White space between thoughts
4. Professional but personal
5. End with question or CTA

### LinkedIn Template: Newsletter → Post

```
[Opening hook - first 2 lines visible before "see more"]

↓

[Problem or situation - relatable to professional audience]

[Your insight or take]

[Brief story or example]

[Key takeaway]

[Question to drive engagement OR specific CTA]

---
[Optional hashtags - 3-5 max]
```

### LinkedIn Formatting

- Line break after each thought
- Use "↓" or "..." for pattern breaks
- Emojis sparingly if at all
- Keep it scannable

## YouTube

### Format Options

**Long-Form Video**
- 8-15 minutes sweet spot
- Best for: Deep dives, tutorials, interviews

**YouTube Shorts**
- Under 60 seconds
- Best for: Quick tips, hooks, clips

### Transformation Rules (Long-Form)

1. Hook in first 30 seconds
2. Open loop early (promise what's coming)
3. Deliver on promise
4. Pattern interrupts every 2-3 minutes
5. Clear CTA at end

### Video Script Template: Article → Video

```
HOOK (0-30 sec)
[Strongest point or surprising statement from article]
"In this video, you'll learn [promise]"

INTRO (30-60 sec)
[Quick credibility + what's coming]

SECTION 1 (2-3 min)
[Main point 1 from article with examples]

SECTION 2 (2-3 min)
[Main point 2 with examples]

SECTION 3 (2-3 min)
[Main point 3 with examples]

SUMMARY (30-60 sec)
[Recap key points]

CTA (30 sec)
[Subscribe + next video + link if relevant]
```

### Shorts Template

```
HOOK (0-3 sec)
[Pattern interrupt or surprising statement]

CONTENT (3-50 sec)
[Single point, tip, or story]

PUNCH (50-60 sec)
[Memorable ending or twist]
```

## Instagram

### Format Options

**Reels**
- 15-60 seconds
- Best for: Tips, hooks, personality

**Carousels**
- Up to 10 slides
- Best for: Education, lists, stories

**Stories**
- 15 seconds per story
- Best for: Behind-scenes, engagement

### Transformation Rules

1. Visual-first platform
2. Text must be readable on mobile
3. Hook in first 2 seconds (Reels)
4. First slide is the hook (Carousels)
5. Accessible language

### Carousel Template: Article → Carousel

```
SLIDE 1: Hook
[Title that creates curiosity - why swipe?]

SLIDES 2-8: Content
[One point per slide]
[Large text, simple visuals]
[Build progressively]

SLIDE 9: Summary
[Quick recap or key takeaway]

SLIDE 10: CTA
[What to do next - save, follow, link in bio]
```

### Reels Adaptation

- Fastest content first
- On-screen text for sound-off viewing
- Trending audio if appropriate
- Direct camera energy

## TikTok

### Format

- 15-60 seconds ideal (up to 3 min)
- Vertical video only
- Sound-on assumed

### Transformation Rules

1. Hook in first 1-2 seconds
2. Authentic > polished
3. Native to platform (not repurposed feel)
4. Trend awareness helps
5. Personality-forward

### TikTok Template

```
HOOK (0-3 sec)
[Pattern interrupt - question, statement, visual]

CONTENT (3-45 sec)
[Get to the point fast]
[One idea only]
[Examples help]

LOOP/END (45-60 sec)
[Satisfying conclusion OR loop back to start]
```

## Podcast/Audio

### Format Options

**Full Episode**
- 20-60 minutes typical
- Best for: Deep conversation, teaching

**Clips**
- 60-90 seconds
- Best for: Social distribution

### Transformation: Article → Podcast

```
INTRO (2-3 min)
[What you'll cover and why it matters]

MAIN CONTENT (15-40 min)
[Article sections become conversation points]
[Add stories and tangents]
[More conversational depth]

OUTRO (2-3 min)
[Summary + what's next]
```

### Clip Selection

Best moments for clips:
- Surprising statements
- Emotional peaks
- Quotable insights
- Laugh moments
- Story climaxes
