---

## Handoff Best Practices

1. **Include full score breakdown** - Downstream skills need context
2. **Mark estimates clearly** - `[SCHATTING]` labels carry through
3. **Specify unique angle** - Critical for differentiation
4. **Provide structure hints** - Based on search intent analysis
5. **Note authority anchors** - Available data/research to reference
