# Lead Magnet Hooks

The hook is why someone trades their email. This reference covers hook formulas, examples, and how to strengthen weak hooks.

---

## Hook Formula Library

### 1. Speed Hook
**Formula:** Get [result] in [short time]

**Examples:**
- "Write Your Sales Page in 60 Minutes"
- "Land Your First Client This Week"
- "Create a Month of Content in One Day"

**Best for:** Time-starved audiences, simple processes

---

### 2. Ease Hook
**Formula:** The simple/easy way to [desired outcome]

**Examples:**
- "Email Marketing Without the Tech Headaches"
- "The Lazy Person's Guide to Lead Generation"
- "Pricing Made Simple: A One-Page Framework"

**Best for:** Overwhelmed audiences, complex topics

---

### 3. Secret Hook
**Formula:** The [hidden/little-known] method for [result]

**Examples:**
- "The Algorithm Loophole Top Creators Use"
- "What $10M Coaches Know About Pricing"
- "The Outreach Strategy Nobody's Teaching"

**Best for:** Insider knowledge, contrarian methods

---

### 4. Mistake Hook
**Formula:** [X] costly mistakes killing your [result]

**Examples:**
- "7 Email Mistakes Costing You Sales"
- "The #1 Pricing Error Consultants Make"
- "Why Your Landing Page Isn't Converting (And How to Fix It)"

**Best for:** Problem-aware audiences, common failures

---

### 5. Checklist Hook
**Formula:** Everything you need to [achieve goal]

**Examples:**
- "The Complete Product Launch Checklist"
- "Never Forget a Step: Content Publishing Checklist"
- "The 47-Point SEO Audit Checklist"

**Best for:** Process-oriented tasks, quality assurance

---

### 6. Template Hook
**Formula:** Copy my [proven template] for [result]

**Examples:**
- "Steal My Cold Email Template (40% Response Rate)"
- "The Exact Proposal Template I Use for $10K Projects"
- "My Content Calendar Template (Copy It)"

**Best for:** DIYers, people who want shortcuts

---

### 7. Case Study Hook
**Formula:** How [person/company] achieved [specific result]

**Examples:**
- "How We Grew to 50K Subscribers Without Ads"
- "Inside the Launch That Made $247K in 72 Hours"
- "How Sarah Went From $0 to $20K/Month in 6 Months"

**Best for:** Social proof, specific strategies

---

### 8. Tool Hook
**Formula:** The [free tool] that [delivers benefit]

**Examples:**
- "ROI Calculator: See If This Tool Is Worth It"
- "Content Idea Generator: Never Run Out of Topics"
- "Pricing Calculator: Know Your Worth"

**Best for:** Interactive value, personalized output

---

### 9. Compilation Hook
**Formula:** [X] best [resources/examples/ideas] for [goal]

**Examples:**
- "50 Subject Lines That Got 50%+ Open Rates"
- "The Best Sales Page Examples (Annotated)"
- "100 LinkedIn Post Hooks That Work"

**Best for:** Inspiration, research shortcuts

---

### 10. Framework Hook
**Formula:** The [name] framework for [achieving outcome]

**Examples:**
- "The P.A.S.T.A. Framework for Content That Converts"
- "The 6 Circles Method for Content Planning"
- "My 3-Step System for High-Ticket Sales Calls"

**Best for:** Methodologies, teachable systems

---

## Hook Strength Test

Rate your hook on each factor (1-3):

| Factor | Score 1 | Score 2 | Score 3 |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|
| Specific | Vague benefit | Somewhat specific | Exact outcome |
| Immediate | Long-term payoff | Medium timeline | Fast results |
| Desirable | Nice to have | Useful | Must have |
| Credible | Sounds too good | Believable | Proven |

**Total Score:**
- 10-12: Strong hook, proceed
- 7-9: Needs refinement
- Below 7: Rethink completely

## Hook Strengthening Techniques

### Add Specificity
**Weak:** "Get More Leads"
**Strong:** "Add 50 Qualified Leads to Your Pipeline Monthly"

### Add Speed
**Weak:** "Improve Your Writing"
**Strong:** "Write Better Headlines in 15 Minutes"

### Add Proof
**Weak:** "Email Templates That Work"
**Strong:** "Email Templates That Get 40%+ Response Rates"

### Add Stakes
**Weak:** "Marketing Mistakes to Avoid"
**Strong:** "7 Marketing Mistakes That Cost You $10K+ Yearly"

### Add Intrigue
**Weak:** "Content Strategy Guide"
**Strong:** "The Content Strategy That Built a $5M Business (With No Ads)"

## Hook Mistakes

### Too Vague
❌ "Marketing Tips and Tricks"
✅ "The 3-Step Framework for Writing Headlines That Convert"

### Too Broad
❌ "The Ultimate Business Guide"
✅ "How to Price Your Services (Without Undercharging)"

### No Transformation
❌ "Email Marketing Best Practices"
✅ "From 10% to 40% Open Rates: Email Subject Line Swipe File"

### Unbelievable
❌ "Make $100K in 30 Days with No Experience"
✅ "How I Landed 3 Clients in My First Month Freelancing"

### Boring
❌ "Resource Guide"
✅ "The Swipe File Behind $2.4M in Sales"

## Hook Testing

### A/B Test Headlines
Create 2-3 hook variations and test:
- Which gets higher opt-in conversion?
- Which attracts better-qualified leads?

### Social Test
Post the hook on social media:
- Does it generate engagement?
- Do people ask for it?

### Verbal Test
Say the hook out loud:
- Does it sound compelling?
- Would you want it?

### Clarity Test
Show to someone unfamiliar:
- Do they understand the value in 5 seconds?
- Can they repeat back what they'd get?

## Hook by Audience Awareness

Match hook intensity to audience awareness:

**Unaware:** Don't know they have a problem
→ Use curiosity/intrigue hooks

**Problem-Aware:** Know the problem, not solutions
→ Use mistake/problem hooks

**Solution-Aware:** Know solutions exist
→ Use method/framework hooks

**Product-Aware:** Know your solution
→ Use proof/case study hooks

**Most Aware:** Ready to buy
→ Use speed/ease hooks (they want shortcuts)
