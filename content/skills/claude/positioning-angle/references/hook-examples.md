# Hook Examples by Framework

Real examples of hooks generated from each framework. Use as inspiration, not templates.

---

## Contrarian Hooks

**Pattern**: "Everyone says [X]. They're wrong. Here's why [Y] actually works."

### Examples

"Stop creating more content. Start distributing what you have."

"The best marketing doesn't feel like marketing."

"You don't need more traffic. You need better conversion."

"Forget the funnel. Build a flywheel."

"Your competitors' strategy won't work for you. Here's what will."

"The most successful founders I know don't have morning routines."

"SEO isn't dead. Your approach to it is."

---

## Enemy Hooks

**Pattern**: "The real problem isn't [surface issue]. It's [enemy]. Here's how to fight it."

### Examples

"Complex software is costing you hours every week. We built the simple alternative."

"Your tech stack is holding you back. One tool to replace twelve."

"Agencies want you dependent. We want you capable."

"Stop letting spreadsheets run your business."

"Your CRM shouldn't require a certification to use."

"Generic AI content is flooding your market. Stand out by sounding human."

"The hustle-harder advice is burning you out. Work smarter, not longer."

## Before/After Hooks

**Pattern**: "From [painful state] to [desired state] in [timeframe]."

### Examples

"From scattered notes to published article in 30 minutes."

"From 'what should I write about?' to 6 months of content planned."

"From dreading sales calls to looking forward to them."

"From guessing what works to knowing exactly what converts."

"From writing all day to writing for an hour. Better results."

"From feeling invisible online to becoming the go-to expert in your niche."

"From inbox chaos to inbox zero by lunch."

"Netflix for your professional development."

"Stripe, but for contracts."

"What Figma did for design, we're doing for content."

"Like autocomplete, but for your entire marketing strategy."

"The 3-step system behind $2.4M in course sales."

"The 5-email sequence that converts at 12%."

## Combination Hooks

### Contrarian + Specificity
"Forget posting daily. One strategic piece per week outperforms 7 mediocre ones by 340%."

### Enemy + Before/After
"Stop fighting your CRM. Go from 'I hate Mondays' to 'I closed three deals before lunch.'"

### Analogy + Outcome
"Grammarly for your marketing copy—content that converts, every time."

### Simplification + Specificity
"Replace 7 tools with 1. Save 12 hours per week."

### Category + Contrarian
"This isn't content marketing—it's content-led growth. And no, you don't need to post every day."

---

## Hook Strength Checklist

Strong hooks have:

- [ ] **Clarity**: Understood in under 3 seconds
- [ ] **Specificity**: Concrete details, not vague promises
- [ ] **Tension**: Creates curiosity or challenges a belief
- [ ] **Relevance**: Addresses something the audience cares about
- [ ] **Differentiation**: Not what competitors are saying
- [ ] **Credibility**: Feels achievable, not hype

Test each hook against this checklist before committing.
